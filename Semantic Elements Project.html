About Content

Deen Dayal Upadhyaya Grameen Kaushalya Yojana (DDU-GKY) is a skilling and placement program under the Ministry of Rural Development (MoRD) in India. It focuses on equipping rural poor youth with marketable skills to secure sustainable employment. DDU-GKY is part of the larger National Rural Livelihood Mission (NRLM) and aims to transform them into an economically independent workforce.
